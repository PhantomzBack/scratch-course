Why Scratch?

Here's Why!





Scratch is a powerful drag and drop tool which is efficient and helps lay the basic foundation for coding. This can help children develop their coding logic and thinking skills. It's intuitive drag and drop design makes it very easy for children to write simple logic programs. Click on the "Why Scratch" button above to check out Scratch yourself.
